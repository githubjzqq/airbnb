关于您的体验达人Ian

I was born in Gangneung, Korea. I've crossed the Straits of Korea from
Japan and I've also sailed to the South Sea and the East Sea from
Tongyeong to Anmok Port. I am an experienced skipper with a formal
yacht certificate and a rescue agent certificate. I provide nice music
on the yacht and I can guide you through the city of Gangneung. I can
also introduce you to rooms, restaurants, and sightseeing spots. You'll
live like a Gangneung local during your time with me.

体验内容
We will sail together on my 28-foot yacht and explore beautiful
Gangneung. You'll have the opportunity to swim, tan, and snorkel
with your own gear. There is no shark or crocodile in God blessing.
You'll enjoy simple snacks and drinks. You can also use the Bluetooth
amplifier to enjoy your favorite music. We'll take in breathtaking views
of the sea from Gyeongpo Beach and Daegwallyeong. It will be a
wonderfully satisfying experience and we'll all enjoy it together.
