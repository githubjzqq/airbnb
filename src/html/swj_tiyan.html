Yacht along the coast with a captain

关于您的体验达人Ian
I was born in Gangneung, Korea. I've crossed the Straits of Korea from Japan and I've also sailed to the South Sea and the East Sea from Tongyeong to Anmok Port. I am an experienced skipper with a formal yacht certificate and a rescue agent certificate. I provide nice music on the yacht and I can guide you through the city of Gangneung. I can also introduce you to rooms, restaurants, and sightseeing spots. You'll live like a Gangneung local during your time with me.
2小时
零食和饮品
以英语和韩语提供
体验内容
We will sail together on my 28-foot yacht and explore beautiful Gangneung. You'll have the opportunity to swim, tan, and snorkel with your own gear. There is no shark or crocodile in God blessing. You'll enjoy simple snacks and drinks. You can also use the Bluetooth amplifier to enjoy your favorite music. We'll take in breathtaking views of the sea from Gyeongpo Beach and Daegwallyeong. It will be a wonderfully satisfying experience and we'll all enjoy it together.
其他应注意的事项
Smoking is not permitted. Your valuables will be kept in private storage. Do not fall into the sea and do wear a life jacket.
体验内包括的东西
free Drinks
I'll provide free (water, mixed coffee, soft drinks.)

free Snacks and sweets
准备物品
Hat, sunglasses, sun block, sneakers
